How to Play

  • Watch the sequence of colors that Simon shows you.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the colored buttons in the same order.
  • Each level adds one more color to the sequence.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends and your score is recorded.
  • Try to beat your high score by reaching the highest level possible!
